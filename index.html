About Dough Delight

Our Story

Dough Delight Live Cake Shop & Bakers began its delicious journey in February 2023 with a simple dream — to bring freshly baked happiness to every celebration and everyday moment. What started as a small offline cake shop soon became a favorite spot for cake lovers who value quality, taste, and creativity.

A popular local bakery and cake shop located in Sayajipura, Vadodara (Gujarat), India. Offers a variety of cakes, pastries, and desserts, and also supports custom cake orders. It’s listed with good customer ratings on food platforms, showing it’s active with regular business.
